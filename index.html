This is a Todo App made by Lalit Rawat

You can write task you want to do and once you have completed the task,you can click on Mark as Done button, then the task will be removed from the todo list.

To have a look at app ,either click on ✖ mark or click on back page.

Persistance of todos - feature is not implemented i.e. no todo is stored in any type of database.
